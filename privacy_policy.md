# Privacy Policy

We keep your data confidential. This policy explains what the bot collects and how it uses it.

## Data Collected
- **Discord IDs**: To track warning logs and request ownership.
- **Form Inputs**: Details you submit in the commission requests, such as budget, payment method, and description.
- **Message IDs**: Stored to scan warnings and prevent duplicates.

## Confidentiality and Sharing
All collected data remains confidential within the Art Commissions server. We never sell or share this data outside the server database.

## Retention and Removal
- Warning logs are scanned and synced for the recent three months.
- Commission request logs are stored in the database.
- Contact server staff to request data removal.
